Home / Campus Life

Life outside the drafting table.

Labs and lectures are half the story — clubs, sport, and residence life fill in the rest.

FAC.01

Fabrication Lab

3D printers, CNC routers, and a full electronics bench — open to any department, any hour of the day.

FAC.02

Central Library

A quiet, wifi-covered reading hall with a full digital journal subscription for every department.

FAC.03

Residence Halls

On-campus housing with study lounges on every floor, five minutes from the main academic block.

FAC.04

Sports Complex

Indoor courts, a running track, and a gym — with inter-department leagues every semester.

FAC.05

Innovation Cell

Seed funding and mentorship for student-founded projects that outgrow the classroom.

FAC.06

Cafeteria

A central dining hall serving three meals a day, steps from every department building.

Sec. B · Student Clubs

Where students build outside the syllabus

Robotics Club

Builds a competition-ready bot every year.

Coding Circle

Weekly problem-solving and hackathon prep.

Design Guild

UI/UX and product design practice group.

Civic Build Society

Community infrastructure micro-projects.

Sec. C · Annual Calendar

Two festivals, every year

Technovate — Tech Fest

A three-day showcase of student capstones, hackathons, and robotics competitions, open to other colleges.

Confluence — Cultural Fest

Music, art, and performance — organised entirely by the student union each spring.

SC SEA College
