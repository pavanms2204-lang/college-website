Five steps from interest to enrolment.

The process is linear by design — no waiting on unclear next steps.

01

Submit eligibility details

Fill the enquiry form below with your academic scores and department preference.

02

Document verification

Upload your marksheets and identity proof for our admissions office to verify.

03

Entrance assessment

Sit the department-specific aptitude round, held on campus or online.

04

Counselling & seat allotment

Meet with faculty advisors to confirm your department and section.

05

Fee payment & enrolment

Complete payment to lock your seat and receive your student ID.
